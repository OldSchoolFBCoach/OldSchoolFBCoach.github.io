Overview

Take control of a college football program and lead the team to victory! Old School Football Coach gives you the true college football coach experience. Set your strategy, pick your plays, and watch the action unfold in front of you. Get ready for full game simulations with complex AI behaviors, presented in a retro 2d style.

Want to build a team from the ground up? Old School Football Coach has a career mode that allows you to grow your team year-after-year on your quest to become the next college football dynasty. Set depth charts, recruit new players, and design new plays on your road to be the #1 team in the nation!

Features

  • Design your league with editable teams and conferences. Decide which team you want to coach.
  • Each player is unique with 15+ attributes that determine how good they are! Review your players and set your depth chart.
  • Choose if you want to control your players directly or take a more relaxed approach by only calling out the plays.
  • Design new plays to outsmart the competition.
  • Postseason bowls and playoffs are highly customizable! Configure the number of spots available as well as conference tie-ins.
  • Research high school players and compete against other schools as you attempt to recruit them to your team.
  • Browse stats and rankings from around the league.

Build 2021-04 and New Trailer

Announcement
April 03, 2021 (Link)

A new early access development build (2021-04) of Old School Football Coach is now publicly available! Notable changes include:

Postseason Games

There is now support for postseason games and they are highly customizable! You can configure the number of playoff spots available in your league and decide of if only the top-ranked teams make it in, or if conference champions have an automatic bid. You can also decide if you want to create your own bowl games and how teams get selected for them as well.

Other Improvements and Fixes

  • Performance optimizations have been put in place when calculating team rankings between weeks. This reduces the time spent waiting for a week to complete and the next week to start. The performance of these calculations scale with the number of teams in the league, so it is now more feasible to have leagues with a larger number of teams in them. -Some minor visual enhancements have been added to the field and player animations. For example, there is now an animation for when a tackled player stands back up.
  • Various bug fixes.

Be sure to check out the new gameplay trailer, best viewed in full screen mode!
