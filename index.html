Overview

Take control of a college football program and lead the team to victory! Old School Football Coach is a PC game that gives you the true college football coach experience. Set your strategy, pick your plays, and watch the action unfold in front of you. Get ready for full game simulations with complex AI behaviors, presented in a retro 2d style.

Want to build a team from the ground up? Old School Football Coach has a career mode that allows you to grow your team year-after-year on your quest to become the next college football dynasty. Set depth charts, recruit new players, and design new plays on your road to be the #1 team in the nation!

Features

  • Design your league with editable teams and conferences. Decide which team you want to coach.
  • Each player is unique with 15+ attributes that determine how good they are! Review your players and set your depth chart.
  • Choose if you want to control your players directly or take a more relaxed approach by only calling out the plays.
  • Design new plays to outsmart the competition.
  • Postseason bowls and playoffs are highly customizable! Configure the number of spots available as well as conference tie-ins.
  • Research high school players and compete against other schools as you attempt to recruit them to your team.
  • Browse stats and rankings from around the league.

Build 21-12 Available - Timeouts, Touchbacks, & More

Announcement
December 01, 2021 (Link)

A new update (21-12) of Old School Football Coach is here! The new changes include the following:

New Features:

  • Timeouts have been implemented! It’s now possible to call a timeout during the game. Calling a timeout allows you to stop the game clock, reset the play clock, and select a new play before the ball is snapped. Timeouts can be called from the game menu.
  • Computer controlled players are now smart enough to take a touchback when appropriate after receiving a kick, interception, or fumble. They now no longer always try to run the ball out of their endzone.
  • Players now celebrate after scoring.

Improvements / Tweaks / Bug Fixes

  • The ESC key will now launch the in-game menu while playing a football game
  • The simulation dialog is now more responsive while running a deep simulation.
  • The game clock now correctly stops when there is a change in possession

Happy coaching!
