Website Launch

Announcement
August 01, 2020 (Link)

This is the new website for Old School Football Coach! Old School Football Coach is an in-development American college football PC game in the sports management simulation genre. You can call your team’s plays and do your best to lead them to victory. For a more in-depth experience, the game also offers a career mode where you can recruit new players, set up depth charts, and other activities you would expect from a college football coach.

I intend to add additional content to this site over time, such as pictures, videos, and additional blog posts as development continue. Stay tuned!
