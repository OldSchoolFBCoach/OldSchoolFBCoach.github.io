Build 22-05 - Career Mode News Headlines

Announcement
May 11, 2022 (Link)

A new update (22-05) of Old School Football Coach is here! The new changes include the following:

Career Mode News Headlines

It’s now easier than ever to stay up to date on what’s going on around your league. Each week new media headlines will be generated and displayed on the revamped career mode home screen. This will help you keep up with things like important games, major upsets, and who’s pulling in the top recruiting classes.

Other Improvements

  • In career mode there is now a menu that will show all of the games happening that week.
  • The team ranking formulas have been slightly tweaked to lessen the wild swings that were happening in the early season
  • Various other minor changes

Happy Coaching!
